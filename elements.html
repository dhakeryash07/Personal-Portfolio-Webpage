ISRO-SAC & MathWorks

Indian Space Research Organization – Space Applications Centre (ISRO-SAC) Internship (Sept. 2023 – Nov. 2023): During my internship at ISRO-SAC, I worked on real-time positioning systems and navigation technologies. I implemented a Kalman filter framework in C for GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) applications, which significantly improved positioning accuracy by reducing errors in real-time measurements. Additionally, I led the GNSS position initialization project, integrating satellite position data, orbital mechanics, and ionospheric delay information. This effort led to a reduction in positioning errors by 20-25%. My work contributed to the enhancement of satellite-based navigation systems, which are critical for various space missions and terrestrial navigation applications.

MathWorks Internship (May 2023 – Sept. 2023): At MathWorks, I gained hands-on experience in numerical computation, data analysis, and data visualization using MATLAB tools. I applied MATLAB’s computational and visualization capabilities to solve complex engineering and scientific problems. My tasks included developing algorithms for data-driven decision-making, analyzing large datasets, and presenting results in visually interpretable formats. The internship also involved working on AI-related projects where I applied machine learning techniques to improve models for various engineering applications. This exposure allowed me to enhance my technical skills in a corporate environment and deepen my understanding of MATLAB’s potential for engineering and data science applications.
